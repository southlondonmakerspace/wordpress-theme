Events

Come along to our next shindig, have a nosey round the space and have a natter with our members...

Open Evening

Wednesday from 19:30 Public Event

Join us for our weekly open evening event, open to the public. This event is a great way to view what the space has to offer, meet and ask those burning questions about how it all works!

μMeet

Thursday from 19:30 Members Only

μMeet, a.k.a. micro(controller) meet(up) is a weekly meetup for Makerspace members, where those from beginner to advanced expertise can come along with their projects and work in the company of others, and debate important topics such as Arduino vs. Raspberry Pi and Arduino vs. Arduino.

Restart Project

Saturday from 12:30 Public Event

Broken lamp? Smashed phone? Lackluster laptop? Makerspace hosts the folks from Restart Project who can help you repair your broken electrical items.

Make the Space

Sunday from 11:00 Public Event

Help us with the ongoing construction work within the Makerspace. Every Sunday we gather those with expertise ranging from builder to beginner to work on the space, have a lunchtime knees up, and learn DIY skills while making the space.

Elephant & Castle Mini Maker Faire 2016

23rd March 2016 External Event

South London Makerspace will be exhibiting at Elephant & Castle Mini Maker Faire 2016.
